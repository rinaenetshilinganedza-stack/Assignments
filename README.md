# 📊 Business Analytics Terminology Project

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is a research-based Business Analytics glossary created as part of the **Business Analytics — Foundations** module.

The objective of this project was to build a strong understanding of the language used by analysts, marketers, and business decision-makers.
It covers key analytics concepts, performance metrics, and marketing KPIs used in modern data-driven organizations.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Understand core **Business Analytics concepts**
* Distinguish between commonly confused terms (e.g., Metric vs KPI)
* Apply analytics terminology to real-world business situations
* Develop data literacy and analytical thinking skills

---

## 📚 Topics Covered

### 1️⃣ Foundational Analytics Concepts

* Business Analytics
* Data Analytics
* Business Intelligence (BI)
* Data-Driven Decision Making

* Big Data & Data Literacy

### 2️⃣ Data, Measurement & Reporting

* Structured vs Unstructured Data

* Data Mining
* Data Visualization
* Dashboards, Metrics & KPIs

### 3️⃣ Customer & Sales KPIs

* Conversion Rate

* Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
* Churn & Retention Rate
* Net Promoter Score (NPS)
* Average Order Value (AOV)
* Sales Funnel Analysis

### 4️⃣ Marketing & Campaign Analytics

* Marketing Campaign Analysis
* ROI & ROAS
* Click-Through Rate (CTR)
* Bounce Rate
* A/B Testing
* Lead Generation
* Marketing Attribution
